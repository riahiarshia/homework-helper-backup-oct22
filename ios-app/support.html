Frequently Asked Questions

How does the AI tutoring work?
Our AI analyzes your homework problems across all subjects and provides step-by-step solutions with explanations. Simply take a photo of your homework or type the problem, and our AI will guide you through the solution process.
What subjects are supported?
Homework Helper supports all subjects for grades K-12, including mathematics, science, English, history, social studies, foreign languages, and more. Our AI can help with homework across all academic disciplines.
Is my data safe and private?
Absolutely! We use industry-standard encryption to protect your data. Your homework problems and progress are kept secure and are never shared with third parties. Read our Privacy Policy for more details.
Can I cancel my subscription anytime?
Yes! You can cancel your subscription at any time through your Apple ID settings. Your access will continue until the end of your current billing period.
What devices are supported?
Homework Helper works on iPhone and iPad running iOS 14.0 or later. We're optimized for all screen sizes and support both portrait and landscape orientations.
How accurate is the AI?
Our AI is highly accurate for most K-12 homework problems across all subjects. However, we recommend double-checking solutions for complex problems and using the app as a learning tool rather than just copying answers.

Troubleshooting Common Issues

App Won't Open or Crashes

  • Make sure you have a stable internet connection
  • Restart the app by closing it completely and reopening
  • Restart your device
  • Update to the latest version from the App Store
  • If problems persist, delete and reinstall the app

Subscription Issues

  • Check your Apple ID subscription settings
  • Verify payment method is valid
  • Ensure you're signed in with the correct Apple ID
  • Wait a few minutes after purchase for activation
  • Contact Apple Support if billing issues persist

AI Not Responding

  • Check your internet connection
  • Make sure the math problem is clearly visible in photos
  • Try typing the problem instead of using camera
  • Ensure you have an active subscription or trial
  • Restart the app if responses are slow

Camera Issues

  • Grant camera permissions in Settings > Privacy > Camera
  • Ensure good lighting when taking photos
  • Keep the device steady while capturing
  • Make sure the math problem fills most of the frame
  • Try using the flash if lighting is poor

Tips for Best Results

Taking Good Photos

  • Use good lighting (natural light is best)
  • Keep your device steady
  • Make sure the entire problem is visible
  • Avoid shadows or glare on the paper
  • Ensure text is clear and readable

Getting Better AI Responses

  • Be specific about what you need help with
  • Ask for step-by-step explanations
  • Request alternative solution methods
  • Ask follow-up questions if something isn't clear
  • Use the chat feature for detailed explanations
  • Specify the subject area for more targeted help

Making the Most of Your Subscription

  • Practice regularly to improve your academic skills across all subjects
  • Review the step-by-step solutions carefully
  • Use the app to check your work on practice problems
  • Explore different solution methods for the same problem
  • Track your progress across all subjects in the app
